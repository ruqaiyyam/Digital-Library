*1984* is a powerful dystopian novel that paints a chilling picture of a totalitarian society under constant surveillance. The story follows Winston Smith, a man living in a world controlled by the Party, led by the mysterious Big Brother. As Winston begins to question the Party's oppressive rule and the rewriting of history, he embarks on a dangerous journey of rebellion. Through its gripping narrative, the novel explores themes of freedom, individuality, and the perils of absolute power.

"The Monk Who Sold His Ferrari" by Robin Sharma tells the story of Julian Mantle, a successful lawyer who experiences a life-changing event. After a heart attack, he decides to give up his luxurious lifestyle and journey to India to discover the secrets of happiness and spiritual fulfillment. The book follows his transformation from a man focused solely on material success to someone who seeks inner peace and purpose. Through a series of lessons, Sharma imparts wisdom on living a balanced life, including principles like mastering one's mind, living with purpose, and achieving success without compromising one's happiness.

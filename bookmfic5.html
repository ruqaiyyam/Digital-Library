Sherlock Holmes is one of the most iconic fictional detectives, created by Arthur Conan Doyle. He is known for his sharp intellect, keen observation skills, and ability to solve complex cases. The first novel, "A Study in Scarlet" introduces Holmes and his faithful companion, Dr. John Watson, as they tackle cases that often involve solving seemingly impossible mysteries. With remarkable attention to detail and a logical approach, Holmes' adventures span multiple novels and short stories, involving criminals, conspiracies, and deep psychological puzzles. Holmes' character has since become a cultural icon, appearing in numerous adaptations and spin-offs.

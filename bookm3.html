The Woman in the Window by A.J. Finn is a gripping psychological thriller that follows Anna Fox, an agoraphobic woman who spends her days drinking wine, watching old movies, and spying on her neighbors. One day, she witnesses something shocking in her neighbor's house, but her condition and her unreliable memory make it difficult for anyone to believe her. As the truth unravels, Anna confronts secrets about her own life and the mysterious events unfolding around her. The novel is a masterful blend of suspense, psychological depth, and unexpected twists.

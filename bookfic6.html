The Hate U Give by Angie Thomas follows Starr Carter, a 16-year-old African American girl who witnesses the police shooting of her childhood friend, Khalil. As the only witness, Starr is thrust into the spotlight, where her testimony becomes a key factor in the fight for justice. The novel tackles issues such as systemic racism, police brutality, and the intersection of race and class. It explores Starr's journey as she navigates the pressures of her community, her family, and her own identity while confronting the harsh realities of life in a divided society.

*Beneath a Scarlet Sky* by Mark Sullivan is a historical novel based on the true story of Pino Lella, an Italian teenager who becomes a reluctant spy during World War II. After losing his family in the chaos of the war, Pino works as a chauffeur for a Nazi general and is involved in the Italian resistance. As the war intensifies, Pino is forced to confront the moral complexities of survival and the choices he makes in a time of devastating conflict. This powerful and gripping novel explores themes of courage, sacrifice, and the resilience of the human spirit.

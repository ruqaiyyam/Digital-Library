"Think Like a Monk" by Jay Shetty takes readers on a journey of spiritual growth, teaching life-changing habits that promote peace, clarity, and fulfillment. Drawing on his experiences as a former monk, Shetty shares his insights on how to quiet the mind, manage stress, and gain purpose in life. The book is divided into practical sections, focusing on key principles like letting go of negativity, embracing gratitude, and creating meaningful relationships. Shetty combines ancient wisdom with modern psychology to help individuals find inner peace and achieve personal success. Through meditation, mindfulness, and self-reflection, readers learn how to lead with intention and think like a monk in their everyday lives.
